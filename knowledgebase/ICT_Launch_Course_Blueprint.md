# ICT Launch Course Blueprint

Attendance: Present, Absent, Late with minutes, Excused. Teacher dashboard includes a copy-friendly EDU Page table.

Review: Pending, Complete, Needs Work, Incomplete, with written feedback and 0-100 score.
